GENERATING EXCELLENT SERVICE

Like you, we have the best interests of the communities we serve in mind. As a community bank with local decision-making, we can help you with the simplest and most complex financial needs. And, our bankers make it a priority to stay on top of the most recent news, trends, rules and regulations your industry faces. In an industry where efficiency is necessary to keep your business thriving, having a team of expert bankers can help you continue running a smooth operation. With expertise in utility services, including power generation and distribution, we are a leading provider of financial solutions to support constant changes, expansion and connectivity needs of the utility industry.

POWERED BY COMMITMENT

Your business has a commitment to service excellence and delivering high-quality energy and resources to residential, commercial and industrial customers. Our team has a commitment to being your financial partner, providing you best practices, services and support to achieve your goals in an ever-transforming industry.

When you’re servicing both consumers and businesses, having a solid financial strategy is crucial to success. A wide range of payment methods provides convenience to you and your customers, stabilizes cash flow and helps process the payments more efficiently. Plus, with equipment financing, term loans and more, we can help fund new facilities or expansions, land investments, infrastructure, equipment, and innovative technology systems that will improve business operations and increase productivity. Our team of experts will be your partner in success so you can focus on the growth of your company.

  • Finance expansions of distribution infrastructure to connect new customers, expand service into new areas, and upgrade technology systems with Working Capital and Financing solutions
  • Mitigate unforeseen variables and manage cash flow with Deposit and Operating Account solutions
  • Streamline payment processes and add a recurring revenue stream with Payment Solutions
  • Optimize and streamline your payables and receivables strategies with Treasury Management
